Our Story

Founded in 2024, Dona's Candles began as a small passion project in a home kitchen. What started as creating candles for friends and family has grown into a dedicated craft focused on sustainability, quality, and beautiful design.

We continue to hand-pour each candle in our studio, using the same careful attention to detail that we started with.
