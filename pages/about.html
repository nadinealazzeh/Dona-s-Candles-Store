Our Story

From a small passion project to a dedicated craft focused on sustainability and quality.

How We Started

Founded in 2024, Candle Store began as a small passion project in a home kitchen. What started as creating candles for friends and family has grown into a dedicated craft focused on sustainability, quality, and beautiful design.

Our founder, Dona Alazzeh, discovered her love for candle making while seeking natural alternatives to commercial candles that often contain synthetic fragrances and harmful chemicals. After months of experimentation with different wax blends, wicks, and essential oils, she developed our signature soy wax formula.

We continue to hand-pour each candle in our studio, using the same careful attention to detail that we started with. Every candle tells a story of craftsmanship, passion, and commitment to quality.

Our Values

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly practices, from sourcing ingredients to packaging. Our candles are made with renewable soy wax and packaged in recyclable materials.

Quality

Each candle is carefully crafted with premium ingredients. We never compromise on quality, ensuring a clean burn and exceptional fragrance throw.

Community

We believe in supporting our local community. We source ingredients locally when possible and partner with nearby artisans and businesses.

Wellness

Our candles are designed to enhance your well-being. We use only essential oils and natural fragrances to create scents that uplift and relax.
