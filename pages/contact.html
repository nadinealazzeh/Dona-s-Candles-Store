Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles typically burn?

Our candles burn for approximately 40-50 hours, depending on the size and burning conditions. For optimal burn time, keep the wick trimmed to 1/4 inch and allow the candle to create a full melt pool on the first burn.

Are your candles vegan and cruelty-free?

Yes! All our candles are made with 100% plant-based soy wax and are never tested on animals. We use cotton wicks and essential oils for fragrance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within Palestine only. We're working on expanding our shipping options in the future.

Can I return a candle if I don't like the scent?

We want you to love your purchase. If you're not satisfied with your candle, we offer returns within 14 days of purchase. The candle must be unused and in its original packaging.
